Arden Wang is a Chinese-American born, Beijing raised creative
from Queens, New York

She is currently a rising junior at Fordham University at Lincoln Center completing a double-major in English and New Media & Digital Design (concentration in Art, Text, and Design), plus a minor in Marketing. She is fluent in English and Mandarin, and has conversational Japanese skills. Arden is a lifelong artist with extensive training in drawing and painting, and aspires to study design and work in creative marketing. She is a Visual Arts Editor at Fordham's Bricolage Comparative Literature Journal. Arden is a Leo, lover of persimmons, and dedicated plant parent. In her free time, she enjoys teaching herself German, studying bookbinding techniques, designing and publishing zines with friends, browsing art and design blogs, and reading books on digital media theory and textile design.
